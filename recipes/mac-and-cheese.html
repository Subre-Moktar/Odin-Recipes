This is a recipe for making mac and cheese, which is one of the best dinners you can have
and would be wonderful during a thanksgiving dinner.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cheese (2 lbs)
  • Pasta
  • Spices
    • Black Pepper
    • Salt
    • Garlic Powder
    • Smoked Papprika
    • Chicken Bullion

Cooking Section

  1. Start By filling a pot with water and putting chicken bullion in it.
    Then when its start boiling put in the macaroni. Let it cook and take out when ready.
    You can also start shredding the cheese if needed.

  2. Next you would start to make the roux for the mac and cheese. So use a stick of butter and
    melt it into the pot. Then mix in the flour with the spices that we added, excepted the chicken bullion.
    Then add in the heavy cream. Finally after everything is mixed well add in 1lbs of cheese into the roux.
    You can start up the oven and set it to 425°F and let it heat up.

  3. The final step is mixing the macaroni and the roux in one big pot. Then moving the macaroni into the
    baking pan. Then add in the last pound of cheese to top off the mac and cheese so it can have that cheesy crust.
    Personally I would add in a sprinkle of smoked papprika to the top also to add in more flavor. Then set it into
    the oven for 20-25 minutes.
