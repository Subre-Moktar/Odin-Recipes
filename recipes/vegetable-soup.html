Vegetable Soup

This is a simple vegetable soup that I love making.
It is a healthy tasty option to have.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Corn
  • Green Peas
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Spices
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper
    • Thyme
    • Parsley

Cooking Section

  1. I like to start off with chopping my Carrots, Celery, and Onions. Then putting them all
    into a pot to start.

  2. When the ingredients look soft, I would add in the cut Garlic and let the garlic start to cook.

  3. Afterwards I would add in the vegetable broth to the soup and start cutting up my potatoes into small pieces.
    Adding them after they have all been cut up.

  4. Next I would add in all the spices into the vegetable soup. and let it sit until the potatoes become soft.

  5. Finally I would add in the parsley, corn, and peas into the vegetable soup and let it simmer for a few minutes.
