Salmon with rice

This is my recipe for how I make my own salmon and rice. You would also need an oven since I usually bake my salmon.
It is a quick recipe taking at most 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salmon
  • Cooking Oil
  • Spices
    • Smoked Paprika
    • Black Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder

Cooking Section

  1. Start off by cooking the rice. Since I dont own a rice cooker, I usually use the stove top to cook my rice.
    Don't Forget to wash your rice!

  2. Then you would prep the oven to 425°F and set you salmon aside. Next start by putting the spices on the salmon, mix it in with some oil to help rub it in.
    Then put the salmon into the oven and let it cook for 20 minutes.

  3. Finally take it out and serve it how you like!
